Our Mission

Leeds Comets CIC is a queer-inclusive netball team dedicated to creating a welcoming and supportive space for LGBTQ+ individuals and allies to enjoy the sport. We focus on making netball accessible to players of all skill levels, fostering a sense of community, and promoting both physical and mental well-being through team sport.

Our aim is to break down barriers to participation, celebrate diversity, and provide a safe and inclusive environment where everyone can develop their skills, build confidence, and have fun.
